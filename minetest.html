Minetest

Open Source Minecraft-like game

Minetest is a near-infinite-world block sandbox game and a game engine, inspired by InfiniMiner, Minecraft, and the like. The game is free and open-source, which can be downloaded on Minetest and it is available for several platforms including Windows, OS X, GNU/Linux, Android, or FreeBSD.

Our Team

Individuals in our team are trusted people within the community who are entitled to be granted privileges so that they can help with the maintenance of our servers. Anyone is welcomed to join us and if you want to be part of our team, simply refer to the IRC page to be able to get in contact with us.

Our Servers

In order to encompass the different varieties of game modes that are available, we choose the most common ones for our servers including the creative mode, PvP or otherwise, the survival mode using game mods made by the very Minetest community itself!
