About Us

We are part of the Sauerbraten and Minetest communities and our team has been created for the purpose of hosting multiple servers to serve the communities of multiplayer online gaming. In addition to hosting servers, we always welcome any new members and like to share our knowledge and experience with them, by either teaching them how to create new maps or simply by allowing them to enjoy being together with us.

Should you have any queries regarding us or any other questions you would like to ask about the games, you can always contact us using our IRC channel & Discord server. Feel free to use any of our servers at any time and enjoy your stay! ;)
